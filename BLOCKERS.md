## Escalation Process
If a blocker is not resolved within one business day:
1. Notify project owner and milestone owner.
2. Reassess risk impact in `STATUS.md`.
3. Log escalation details in `BITACORA.md`.
4. Define temporary mitigation or scope adjustment in `PLAN.md`.
5. Track to closure and document resolution plus lessons learned.
